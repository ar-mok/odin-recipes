Easy Japanese Fried Rice

An easy to make fried rice dish

With simple ingredients and equally simple directions, Japanese fried rice is a must have in your meal rotation. Made in just over an hour this is an easy choice either for yourself or for when you have company. This recipe serves 8 but the amounts can easily be scaled larger or smaller.

    Ingredients
  • 2 cups of jasmine rice
  • 2 cups of chicken stock
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 4 table spoons of butter
  • 2 large eggs beaten
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons sesame seeeds, or to taste
  • 2 table spoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
